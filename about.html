About Prank Toolkit

Prank Toolkit is a collection of 30+ interactive prank web pages designed for sharing laughs with friends. Each prank page features a unique interactive mechanic — typically a button that becomes increasingly difficult to click as it flees, teleports, and dodges the cursor — creating a hilarious escalating experience that almost always ends in laughter.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe that the best kind of humor is the kind you share. Our mission is to create the web's most comprehensive collection of harmless, funny prank pages that anyone can share with a single link. No apps to download, no accounts to create — just open, laugh, and share.

👥 Who Is This For?

Prank Toolkit is for anyone who enjoys playful humor with friends. Whether you're in a group chat looking to break the ice, a couple wanting a cute interactive moment, or roommates settling a chore dispute with laughter — our pranks turn everyday social situations into memorable shared experiences.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Prank Toolkit is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools, no server-side code. This keeps the site fast, lightweight, and easy to deploy. All prank pages are self-contained HTML files that run entirely in the browser.

⚠️ Responsible Use

All pranks on this site are designed for harmless fun between friends. They are meant to create laughter, not distress. Please do not use these pranks to harass, bully, or genuinely upset anyone. If someone isn't enjoying the joke, stop. Good pranks end with everyone laughing — including the target.
